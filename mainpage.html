Fun Fact #1

The Apple logo turned multicolored in 1977 to represent the fact that Macs had colored screens.
They then changed the logo back to the iconic white design after, but the marketing definitely seemed to work.

Fun Fact #2

Carolyn Davidson was the designer of the original Nike logo, all the way back in 1971 and she was paid only $35 for it.
Once the company went public, however they did give her a little more in the form of Nike stocks that are worth who knows how much now.

Fun Fact #3

The average attention span has decreased from 12 to 8 seconds
A goldfish, at 9 seconds, has a longer attention span than the average internet user. You must deliver the content to the audience in the shortest amount of time possible. Otherwise, you risk having a high bounce rate and the potential customer leaving to get what they need elsewhere.
